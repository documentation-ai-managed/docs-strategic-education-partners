Personal information is information and data  that can be used to identify you as an individual. We collect your personal information when you interact with a form or input field on the platform or through one of our verified third parties or you provide it to us through the phone, email, or other method of communication.

The personal information and data we may collect about you includes:

- Identifying details: your name, date of birth, profession, and photograph.

- Contact details: your mailing address, telephone number, and email address.

- Professional records: professional employment history and  verification of any required authorizations, licenses, and/or clearances related to your profession.

- Financial details: bank account and transactional details regarding payments to you.

- Platform activity data: technical details including your internet protocol address and geolocation, login data, and browser and device details.

- Communication preferences: email preference for receiving marketing-related newsletters from us. 

- Survey responses: feedback provided to us in the form of surveys or polls regarding platform functionality and your experience with the platform.

# Platform analytics and cookies

We use product analytic gathering tools to track user behavior and activity throughout the platform. These analytic tools utilize cookies to track your actions across sessions. Cookies are text files added to your web browser’s cache that store user behavior and preferences. These cookies do not contain  personally identifiable information but are used to identify you when you return to the platform. You can configure your web browser to block some or all cookies. In doing so, you may diminish the normal functionality of the platform or restrict access to certain features.
