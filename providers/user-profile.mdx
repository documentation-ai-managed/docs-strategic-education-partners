## Accessing your profile

To access your profile, click on your name in the lower left hand corner of the screen and select the **Profile and settings** option. From here you can manage the following settings:&#x20;

- Photo upload

- View your display name

- Change password

- Edit details

## Editing your details

On the edit details page, you can update the following:

- First name

- Last name

- Username\
  Your username is used for your unique therapy room link that you will share with schools and students for sessions. By default, this is based off of your first and last name.

- Display name\
  Your display name is shown in your waiting room and therapy room. You may change it to personalize your waiting room greeting and label within your therapy room.

## Associated schools

Below your profile details is a list of the schools you are currently associated with. This will allow you to create student records for your caseload (see [Student records](/providers/student-records) for more details). If you do not see a school you are working with, please contact [support@septherapy.com](mailto:support@septherapy.com).
