title: Participant video display
---
The main layout of the therapy room is made up of the participant video display. All participants are represented by a video display box which shows the video feed from the primary camera if turned on. Document cameras are also represented by video display boxes if turned on by the participant. The participant's name is always displayed in the lower left hand corner.

## Video display options

Additional control options are available for all participants by clicking the three dots menu in the upper right hand corner. While all participants have control over how their video display box appears, providers have additional options that affect the experience of other participants.

<Card title="Controls providers have for their own video display boxes" horizontal="false" target="_self">
  Providers have the following options for their own video display boxes:

  - Rotate display - rotates the display 90 degrees to the (right?). Displays can be rotated multiple times.

  - Mirror display - flips the display of the video. Helpful for when displaying text that may display backwards on screen.

  - Change display label - allows the provider to change the display label for their video display box. By default, this label uses the display name field from the user profile. This field can be updated to permanently change how a provider's name is displayed in the therapy room.&#x20;
</Card>

<Card title="Controls providers have for other participants" horizontal="false" target="_self">
  Providers have the following options for other participants' video display boxes:

  - Request participant unmute or mute their microphone - allows the provider to ask a participant to mute or unmute their microphone. Participants will see a notification on screen.

  - Hide or show display from participant - allows the provider to hide or show the video display box from the participant while keeping it visible for the provider. When a video display box is hidden, the participant is notified as to avoid them assuming an error is preventing the video from being displayed.&#x20;

  - Rotate display - rotates the display 90 degrees to the (right?). Displays can be rotated multiple times.

  - Mirror display - flips the display of the video. Helpful for when displaying text that may display backwards on screen.

  - Change display label - allows the provider to change the display label for the participant's video display box.&#x20;

  - Dismiss participant - allows the provider to remove the participant from the session.&#x20;
</Card>

## Modifying the video display layout

Providers have the ability to increase or decrease the size of any video display box in the therapy room. &#x20;
