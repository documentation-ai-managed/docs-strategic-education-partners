The therapy room is where you meet with students to conduct services. Every Strategic Education Partners provider is given a unique link for their students to access their therapy rooms. &#x20;

When you first enter your therapy room, you will be prompted to grant access . Depending on your browser, you can choose to save these settings so you won't be prompted.

After this is done, you should see yourself in your camera. This screen allows you to verify your devices are working properly. If you need to make any changes, click on the gear icon to bring up the device settings dialog.&#x20;

When you're ready, click "Continue to therapy room".
